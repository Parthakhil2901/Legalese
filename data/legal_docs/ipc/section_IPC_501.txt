Section IPC_501

Description of IPC Section 501
According to section 501 of Indian penal code, Whoever prints or engraves any matter, knowing or having good reason to believe that such matter is defamatory of any person, shall be punished with simple imprisonment for a term which may extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.


IPC 501 in Simple Words
If someone prints or engraves any content, knowing or having good reason to believe that it is defamatory about a person, they can be punished with up to two years of simple imprisonment, a fine, or both.
